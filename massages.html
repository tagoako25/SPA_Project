Regular massage therapy will boost immunity and guard against injuries and strains that could otherwise happen from too much tension creating structural flaws.

Your nerve system is stimulated and weariness is reduced by massage, which gives you a sense of renewed vitality. It will lessen emotional, mental, and physical stress.

The body can relax if these points are in balance. In order to release the muscles and reach deeper tissues, the massage therapist additionally applies heat to the stones as if they were hand extensions.

Is a holistic therapy based on the idea that the body's reflex areas and zones are mirrored in the feet? Energy channels connect reflex sites, which correspond with all of the body's systems and organs.

A blockage of these pathways results in imbalance or sickness. By applying pressure on reflex points, blockages that prevent energy from flowing freely are intended to be removed, restoring equilibrium to the body.
