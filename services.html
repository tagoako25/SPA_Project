CANCELLING, MODIFYING OR CHANGING APPOINTMENTS

We understand that life can be unpredictable, and sometimes you may need to adjust your spa appointment. Here at Serenity Spa, we want to make the process as convenient as possible for you.

Cancellations

  • We require a minimum of 24 hours notice for cancellation of any spa appointment. This allows us to offer the appointment time to another guest.
  • Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice will be subject to a 50% cancellation fee.
  • You can easily cancel your appointment online through your account, or by calling us at +1 123 1234 5678.

Modifications

  • We understand that you may need to change the time or date of your appointment. We will do our best to accommodate your request, subject to availability.
  • Please notify us of any desired changes to your appointment at least 24 hours in advance.
  • You can modify your appointment online through your account, or by calling us at +1 123 1234 5678.

Late Arrivals

  • We strive to ensure a relaxing and efficient experience for all our guests.
  • If you are running late for your appointment, please call us in advance to let us know.
  • We will do our best to accommodate you, however, late arrivals may result in a shortened appointment time to avoid impacting following appointments.

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation. If you have any questions about our cancellation or modification policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
